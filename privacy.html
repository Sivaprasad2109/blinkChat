SMessage — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17-11-2025

Welcome to SMessage. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how our service operates. This Privacy Policy explains how SMessage handles information within the app and website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SMessage is designed for privacy. We do not collect or store:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Account information or passwords
  • Contact lists or device identifiers
  • Chat messages or media
  • Room keys, encryption keys, or metadata
  • User location or browsing history

2. End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted directly on your device before they are sent, and only the intended recipient can decrypt them. We cannot read, monitor, or access your chat content.

3. Temporary Room Data

To enable chatting, we temporarily process:

  • Randomly generated room IDs
  • Temporary socket session IDs
  • Room expiration timestamps

This data is automatically deleted when the room expires or all users leave. No long-term storage or logging is performed.

4. Local Storage Usage

We only use local storage for:

  • Your display name
  • Basic UI preferences

This information never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services & Ads

SMessage does not currently use ads or third-party SDKs. If advertising (such as Google AdMob) is added in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

6. Acceptable Use

Users must not use SMessage for:

  • Illegal or harmful activities
  • Harassment or abusive content
  • Sharing malicious files or harmful links
  • Attempts to break or bypass encryption/security

7. Children’s Privacy

SMessage is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.
